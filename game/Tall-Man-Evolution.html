Tall Man Evolution by YAD.Com


Reminder that you can change your page size if this game is too big/small for your screen.
Tall Man Evolution is a fun parkour game. Get as high and wide as possible to finally destroy the robots and pass the levels. Make the tall man go to the right gate and become a taller and bigger man. Note that you need to avoid the obstacles or your tall man will be broken. Enjoy the best parkour game ever!
