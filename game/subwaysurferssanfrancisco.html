Let me tell you folks, I just played Subway Surfers San Francisco and it was fantastic, absolutely fantastic. The graphics are top-notch, the gameplay is addictive, and it's a lot of fun. Overall, if you want to have a lot of fun and experience the thrill of running through the streets of San Francisco, play Subway Surfers San Francisco. Believe me, it's a winner.
