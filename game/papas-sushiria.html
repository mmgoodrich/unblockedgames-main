Papa's Sushiria by Flipline Studios


Reminder that you can change your page size if this game is too big/small for your screen.
Papa's Sushiria is an amazing game that lets you experience the life of a sushi chef. You get to make all sorts of delicious sushi for your customers and you can even customize them to their liking. The game is so addictive, you won't be able to put it down! The graphics are amazing and the gameplay is so smooth. It's definitely one of the best restaurant simulation games out there.
