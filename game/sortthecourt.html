Sort the Court is an amazing game, folks. It's one of the most addictive and enjoyable games I've ever played. And let me tell you, I've played a lot of games in my time. The concept is simple: you're a king or queen and you have to make decisions to grow your kingdom. But don't let the simplicity fool you, this game is packed with surprises. The graphics are fantastic, let me tell you.The developers really put a lot of effort into making the game look great. And the music is also top-notch.It really adds to the overall experience. But what really sets this game apart is the decision-making. Every choice you make affects your kingdom and its inhabitants. And you never know what's going to happen next. One wrong decision can lead to disaster, but the right choice can make you a hero. Overall, I highly recommend Sort the Court. It's a fun and addictive game that will keep you coming back for more. And who knows, you might even learn a thing or two about being a great leader.
