Krunker

Krunker is a 3D action FPS, created by Yendis Entertainment and owned by FRVR. Master this highly skilled movement-based game, explore thousands of different custom games and modes and host your custom server.

Join an awesome Community and create mods and maps, with the easy-to-use creation tools, where there are no limits to your creativity.

The game features dedicated servers on every continent, a thriving economy with thousands of items and cosmetics to unlock, where you can sell and trade with players all over the World.

Krunker will guarantee the ultimate fun gameplay experience, deeply rooted in fast-paced and highly-responsive mechanics in which you can play solo, with your friends or clan members.

Krunker is a game with a hardworking development team and a welcoming community.
